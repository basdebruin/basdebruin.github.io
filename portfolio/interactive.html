Interactive Apps

I make interactive experiences that combine user interaction with visuals and sound.

A recent personal project I made is Touch Piano, a web-app for iPad that turns the regular piano layout on it’s head. This creates a fun and novel way of playing chords and melodies that could only exist on a medium like the iPad.

The app supports multiple layouts and 10-finger multitouch. The app is made using html and javascript, using p5js for the UI and sound generation.

Busy is an audio game about being distracted by your phone while riding public transit. The player plays a distracting game on their phone while listening to bus-stops being announced. The player has to juggle their attention between their phone and the bus sounds, this proved to be quite difficult.

The product was made as a school assignment and consists of a web-app and a Max/MSP Patch along with branding and market strategy.
