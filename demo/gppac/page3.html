About GPPAC

Our call to action

We continue to work hard to prevent violent conflict and to foster peace in our communities. We continue to do this by ensuring that all members of society are engaged and participate in this. We believe that conflict prevention is possible if we work together. Therefore, we partner with governments, donors, civil society and businesses. For more detailed information of our work and our stories of impact visit our website.

Meet the Team

Thank you

We thank our donors for making our work possible, for their continued support and continued commitment to the work of GPPAC. Donors are part and parcel in our efforts to build legitimate and sustainable peace in times of violent conflicts being increasingly fragmented and complex.

GPPAC is grateful for the support and generosity of its main donors, the Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Swedish International Development Agency. In 2019 their contribution was yet again instrumental in facilitating cross-regional activities and developing skills and knowledge of GPPAC members.

We appreciate the tremendous support of the German Ministry of Foreign Affairs through their ifa/zivik programme to train a group of peace champions in Uganda to prevent youth re-radicalization in the country.

2019 marked the end of an 18-month project in Kyrgyzstan , funded by the United Nations Peacebuilding Fund. Through the use of the participatory video methodology, a group of young people became agents of change and bridged social, gender and ethnic divides.

Finally, as a testament to our collective efforts and commitment to peacebuilding and conflict prevention, GPPAC was honoured to be awarded the prestigious Luxembourg Peace Prize in July 2019.

Our integrity system

Integrity and accountability are at the heart of GPPAC’s work. So we strive to conduct all our operations with honesty and transparency. In 2019, we started with setting up our integrity system to ensure ethical and responsible conduct throughout the GPPAC network and Global Secretariat.
