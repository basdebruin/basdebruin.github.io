2d Sequence

For a recent school-assignment I made 2d Sequence, a web-app, designed for iPad that plays with the concept of the music sequencer.

With colors representing different tones, players can create melodies by placing colored squares next to eachother.

The UI is made using html elements controlled by Javascript with sounds generated using Tone.js.

I designed and prototyped

“Busy” Audio Game

Busy is an audio game about being distracted by your phone while riding public transit. The player plays a distracting game on their phone while listening to bus-stops being announced. The player has to juggle their attention between their phone and the bus sounds, this proved to be quite difficult.

The product was made as a school assignment and consists of a web-app and a Max/MSP Patch along with branding and market strategy.
